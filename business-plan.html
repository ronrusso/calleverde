CALLE VERDE BUSINESS PLAN

Dream Bigger - Sueña en Grande

1. Company Purpose

Calle Verde exists to preserve, modernize, and monetize Latino cultural heritage through scalable, AI-driven media.

We use AI avatar creators, automated content pipelines, and culturally authentic storytelling to educate, inspire, and unify Latino communities. Our content will initially lead with pop culture news and trending topics, while long-form formats, such as Spanish history in the United States, will be scaled for evergreen IP.

Our core objective is to create a portfolio of ownable, high-value IP and characters from this content foundation.

2. Problem

The high-growth AI influencer market lacks a well-rounded and culturally authentic ecosystem for the global Latino audience. Existing AI influencers primarily focus on fashion, modeling, or narrow niches, leaving a massive white space in the cultural and educational media landscape.

There is no major AI-driven IP universe dedicated to celebrating and educating on Latino heritage. Consequently, despite the massive Latino digital influence, the ownership of scalable, positive, and brand-safe IP and monetization platforms remains external to the community.

3. Solution

Calle Verde solves the white space problem by deploying an AI-first media ecosystem focused on the global Latino audience. We will launch a portfolio of AI-generated Latino and Latina avatar influencers who function as ownable, brand-safe characters to deliver culturally authentic content at scale.

These avatars:

  • Speak native Spanish and bilingual English

  • Operate continuously across YouTube, Shorts, TikTok, and Instagram

  • Are designed as ownable characters, not one-off presenters

Our scalable model creates a powerful cycle: Education becomes entertainment. Entertainment becomes community. Community becomes commerce. The content pipeline is currently operational for voice synthesis and scripting, with full AI avatar generation and multi-platform publishing being phased in Q3/Q4 of 2026.

4. Product

4.1 AI Avatar Media Network

Serialized, short-form entertainment and educational content driven by character-based storytelling.

4.2 Calle Verde IP Universe

A scalable portfolio of named avatar characters with defined personalities, visual identities, and story arcs, serving as the foundation for the entire media network.

4.3 Merchandise & Brand Extensions

Culture-first apparel, accessories, and collectibles tied directly to the characters and themes of the Calle Verde IP Universe.

11. Competitive Landscape – AI Influencers

Overview

The AI influencer market is still in its early stages. While several AI-generated influencers have achieved scale, none currently focus on Latino or Spanish cultural education as a core mission.

Most existing AI influencers fall into one of three categories:

  1. Fashion and modeling

  2. General lifestyle or culture

  3. Narrow educational or advocacy niches unrelated to heritage

This creates a clear opportunity for Calle Verde to define and lead a new category:

AI-driven Latino cultural and historical education with ownable IP.
